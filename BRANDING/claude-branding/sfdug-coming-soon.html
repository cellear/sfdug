Twenty Years of Bay Area Drupal

In April 2006, Zack Rosen and Gregory Heller organized the first Drupal Camp SF — a two-day training at Compumentor's offices with Jeff Robbins from Lullabot. It sold out in five days.

That was the beginning. We're still here.
