# San Francisco Drupal Users Group (SFDUG)

Official website for the San Francisco Drupal Users Group—celebrating over 20 years of Bay Area Drupal community building.

## About SFDUG

The San Francisco Drupal Users Group is one of the longest-running Drupal meetups in the world, bringing together developers, site builders, content strategists, and Drupal enthusiasts in the Bay Area since the early 2000s.

### Community Leadership & Transformation

Under current leadership, SFDUG has modernized from casual meetups to professional monthly sessions:

- **Production-Quality Recordings:** Professional video capture and publishing for wider community access
- **Structured Monthly Sessions:** Regular schedule with diverse topics from beginner to advanced
- **Community Engagement:** Building connections between Bay Area Drupal professionals and newcomers
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Creating lasting resources through recorded presentations and discussions

### What We Do

**Monthly Meetups:**
- Technical presentations and hands-on workshops
- Community discussions and Q&A sessions
- Networking opportunities for Bay Area Drupal professionals
- Beginner-friendly content alongside advanced technical topics

**Topics Covered:**
- Drupal development best practices
- Module and theme development
- Site building and architecture
- Performance optimization
- AI-enhanced workflows
- Migration strategies
- Drupal 11 and new features

**Video Archive:**
Recordings of presentations are published to make knowledge accessible to those who can't attend in person and to create lasting educational resources for the Drupal community.

## This Repository

This repository contains the SFDUG website, built with Drupal CMS. The site serves as:
- Event calendar and meetup information
- Community resource hub
- Archive of past presentations and resources
- Connection point for the Bay Area Drupal community
